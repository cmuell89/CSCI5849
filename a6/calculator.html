About this app

Press 'm' to switch mode between arrow control and strafing control.

Subject: House mate

Arrow Control

Arrows: move selection.
Enter: activates calculator key.

Use the following equations:

8 - 7 * 2 = ?
(12 - 6) * 3 = ?
(3 * 2) + 5 = ?
(6 + 5) / 13 = ?

Total time to complete:
Trial 1 (Eq. 1 & 2): 27
Trial 2 (Eq. 3 & 4): 28
Trial 3 (Any two): 20
--------
Average: 25

Notes: The user found this interface much more usable. She was noticeably quicker.

Strafing Control


Space Bar: switches strafe direction.
Control: changes Speed
